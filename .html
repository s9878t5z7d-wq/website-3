Who We Are

Fort Wayne's Most
Trusted Concrete Crew

Vega Concrete LLC was founded on a simple principle: do the job right, every single time. For over 4 years, we've been pouring concrete across the Fort Wayne metro — from backyard patios to large commercial foundations.

Every project starts with honest communication and a detailed plan. We show up on time, use top-grade materials, and leave your site clean. Our crews are experienced, our work is guaranteed, and our prices are fair.

Whether you need a new driveway, a stamped decorative patio, or a full structural foundation, Vega Concrete has the equipment and expertise to get it done.
